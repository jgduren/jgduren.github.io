Eco-Friendly

Unlike soy, palm and paraffin wax, natural beeswax is used essentially in its native state. There is no bleaching or hydrogenating. Beeswax does not go rancid, has a very long shelf life and wonâ€™t melt or distort. Beeswax production does not require the sole use of large areas of agricultural land.

Air Purifying

100% natural beeswax candles have long been known for their air purifying effects and can greatly reduce dust and other allergens in your home.

Longer Burning

100% pure beeswax naturally burns up to three times longer than paraffin. Many other candle waxes have different degrees of toxic additives to produce longer burn times.

No Synthetic Fragrance

The pure, high quality natural beeswax in Woodinville Waxworks Candles has a natural honey aroma with nothing added. Woodinville Waxworks Candles contain only pure essential oils, no synthetic fragrances.
