Can I custom order?

Of course! All custom orders take two business weeks to complete!

Can you put a bird on it?

We LOVE birds!

Where do you get your beeswax?

Our beeswax is purchased from an organic local bee farm in the San Juan Islands.

What is your return policy?

We offer a full refund within 30 days of the original purchase.

Do your candles have added fragrance?

Our candles our naturally scented- we do not add any oils to scent our candles.

Do you offer candle making supplies?

We are currently working on developing a line of DIY candle-making kits, but unfortunately we do not offer any at the moment.

Where can I place my order?

We accept phone or online orders!
