Target Audience

The persona of my audience is most likely a friend of mine, the age range is probably from 17-30 years old. Most of the people looking
at my page are going to be fellow college students with a few exceptions. Though my friends are probably not interested in my professional endeavors
I am hoping to develop this page into something that I can use in the future with potential employers. At that time my target audience will be
vastly different.

Jennifer is a senior at Willamette University pursuing a Sociology major and a minor in Women's and Gender Studies.
She is also a member of Alpha Chi Omega Sorority- Beta Chi Chapter, which works in the Salem community to promote Domestic Violence
and Sexual Assualt awareness. Jennifer is currently a Domestic Violence Advocate for the Center for Hope and Safety in Salem, OR but has held positions
in the Oregon State Capitol,The King County Prosecuting Attorney's Office, and at Nordstrom. Jennifer is attending law school next fall and intends
to focus on family law.

Inspiration

Here are my favorite personal sites:

Krista Gray
Gray's page is attractive because of it's simple yet efficient design. I like that it looks professional but that it's still easy
to navigate. I am curious as to how you can insert links in the top corner rather than the middle of the page.

Sarah Chang
Sarah Chang's page is aesthetically similar to Krista Gray's. Both are very simple but informative. I like that there is a simple color
scheme and that the text is centered. I also like that Sarah has links to different institutions she is affiliated with at the bottom of the page.

Ian Enders
Ian Enders' page is one that I wish I could call my own. Not only are you able to able to experience his quirkiness, but his page
is efficient and interesting.I enjoy the quick intersting facts and career acheivements rather than a long narrative. I am not sure
how employers outside of a creative field would respond to this page, but I love it.

Callie Schweitzer
I enjoy Schweitzer's page because of the clean layout and the formatting of the text in the center of the page. The text looks clean and
magically frames her picture. I would like to figure out how to format images like this on my personal site.

Allison Stadd
I like Stadd's page because it feels fun, creative, and personal. Having the image of the typewriter in the background gives you some insight
into her career and exudes creativity. This page is still simple, but the friendly text and scrolling font keep this page different and inviting
to it's visitors.
