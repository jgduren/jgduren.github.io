Jennifer is a senior at Willamette University pursuing a Sociology major and a minor in Women's and Gender Studies.
She is also a member of Alpha Chi Omega Sorority- Beta Chi Chapter, which works in the Salem community to promote Domestic Violence and Sexual Assualt awareness.
Jennifer is currently a Domestic Violence Advocate for the Center for Hope and Safety, but has held positions in the Oregon State Capitol,
The King County Prosecuting Attorney's Office, and at Nordstrom. Jennifer is attending law school next fall and intends to focus on family law.
